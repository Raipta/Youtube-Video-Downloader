Questions

Is this free to use?

Yes, completely free. No account, no subscription, no hidden fees.

Why doesn't 1080p always work?

YouTube serves 1080p as two separate streams (video + audio). Our backend merges them server-side using ffmpeg. If it fails, try 720p which is always a single merged stream.

How long are download links valid?

YouTube's CDN links expire after a few hours. If a link stops working, just fetch the video again.

Can I download private or age-restricted videos?

No. Only public videos are supported.
